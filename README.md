# GL Framework: Structural Efficiency Measurement

**GL Framework** is a diagnostic tool developed by **GFI Flow Intelligence** to measure and optimize institutional performance. It moves beyond traditional output metrics by focusing on the structural friction within a system.

## The Formula
The efficiency of a system is defined by the GL equation:
$$GL = \frac{Fs \times Vn}{Pd \times Cf}$$

* **Fs (Flow Speed):** Rate of policy execution.
* **Vn (Verified Net):** Actual delivered outcomes.
* **Pd (Procedural Drag):** Bureaucratic and administrative friction (Ghost GDP).
* **Cf (Compliance Friction):** Complexity of regulatory adherence.

## Application
This framework identifies **Ghost GDP**—the economic activity consumed by internal system friction rather than productive value.
