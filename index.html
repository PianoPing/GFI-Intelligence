What GL Measures

Capital velocity,
structurally verified.

GL does not design transformation. It does not implement automation. It verifies whether capital efficiency actually improved.

01 / Fs

Flow Success Rate

Percentage of eligible processes completing their core workflow without friction-induced failure or escalation.

02 / Vn

Strategic Value Score

Weighted policy and capital impact of each structural layer — scored 0–10 against transformation objectives.

03 / Pd

Pain Duration

Annual hours consumed navigating structural friction — approvals, rework loops, decision bottlenecks.

04 / Cf

Cognitive Friction Index

Mental load imposed by system complexity. Measured as a normalized 0–10 index across organizational layers.

GL = (Fs × Vn) / (Pd × Cf)   ·   GLresilient = (Fs × Vn) / (Pd × Cf × SRF)     VIEW FULL METHODOLOGY →
