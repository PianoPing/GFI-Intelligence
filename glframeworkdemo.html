Flow Score
How smoothly can a citizen complete the intended policy action? Measures touchpoints, handoffs, and resolution time. Scale 0–10.

Vn
Validity Norm
Is the process grounded in legitimate policy intent? Measures alignment between regulation and lived reality. Scale 0–1.

Pd
Procedural Drag
How much unnecessary friction does the system impose? Redundant steps, opaque rules, poor sequencing. Scale 1–10.
×
Cf
Compliance Friction
Cost burden on citizens to meet requirements — financial, cognitive, and time cost of proof and compliance. Scale 1–10.
